Briefly summarize the requirements and goals of the app you developed. What user needs was this app designed to address?
The requirements for the app are that it can have a set weight and a goal weight to be logged so that users can track their weight progress, be it losing or gaining.

What screens and features were necessary to support user needs and produce a user-centered UI for the app? How did your UI designs keep users in mind? Why were your designs successful?
Features for the app were kept at a minimum so that there were less screens the user needed to go through to access relevant information/update information. The design itself kept in mind that it should be usable one handed and be able to take advantage of common industry standards for layout design to make it as intuitive as possible.

How did you approach the process of coding your app? What techniques or strategies did you use? How could those techniques or strategies be applied in the future?
The process of coding my app is like that of all my other projects. Iterative. I have a good sense of what I want and how to accomplish it but research is required. I may look to better tools to assist in that research as I did come across issues with what was needed for the programming.

How did you test to ensure your code was functional? Why is this process important, and what did it reveal?
I ensured my code was as functional as I could make it while maintaining certain design principles that I have been honing and learning over the past few years and this extends to testing that code through iterations and step by step testing. This testing is vital to isolate bugs and find where a redesign may be required if something turns out non functional.

Consider the full app design and development process from initial planning to finalization. Where did you have to innovate to overcome a challenge?
I had to do much in the way of research to get the design working, particularly with XML files as I have the least experience with that. The java side was fairly straightforward, though some research was needed there as well so that I could overcome any of the challenges I faced with the API.

In what specific component of your mobile app were you particularly successful in demonstrating your knowledge, skills, and experience?
I would say the design of the app is what I am most proud of. It demonstrates my understanding of how to scale an app to that size and requirements as well as have a good eye for what i necessary to do so.
